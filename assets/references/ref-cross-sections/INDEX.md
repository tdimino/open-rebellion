# Incredible Cross-Sections (1998)

Full scan of *Star Wars: Incredible Cross-Sections — The Ultimate Guide to Star Wars Vehicles and Spacecraft* by David West Reynolds, illustrated by Hans Jenssen and Richard Chasemore (DK Children, 1998).

**Purpose**: Gold-standard ship technical diagrams. Feed individual page crops as Gemini reference images when upscaling GOKRES ship status views, damage diagrams, and weapon arc displays.

**Source**: Anna's Archive (annas-archive.gl)

**Files**: `incredible-cross-sections-1998.pdf` (13MB, 36 pages)

**Extracted**: 33 page images at 288 DPI (PyMuPDF zoom=3) as `page_0001.png` through `page_0033.png`.

**Usage**: Use individual page PNGs as Gemini reference images alongside the source BMP for ship diagram upscaling.
