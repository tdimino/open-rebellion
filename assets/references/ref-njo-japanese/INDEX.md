# Japanese New Jedi Order Covers — Tsuyoshi Nagano

## Style Notes

- Painterly digital art with visible brushwork
- Dramatic rim lighting and lens flare
- Warm amber/gold highlights against cool blue/purple shadows
- Characters rendered with cinematic realism (not anime)
- Action compositions with strong diagonal lines
- Space battles and lightsaber duels rendered with motion blur
