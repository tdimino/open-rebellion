# Galactic Battlegrounds Concept Art

25 concept art images from Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds (2001, LucasArts/Ensemble Studios).

**Purpose**: Same-era LucasArts game art as style reference. Pre-rendered buildings, units, and environments share the visual language of Rebellion's facility and unit views.

**Source**: MixnMojo LucasArts Art Archive (mixnmojo.com/media/galleries/)

**Naming**: `gb-{timestamp}.jpg` (original MixnMojo asset timestamps)
